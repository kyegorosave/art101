Challenge

The challenge of this lab is to confidently be able to plan out your code through the method of pseudocoding. By doing so, you are able to plan things out before you start your actual coding, giving you a better basis to work off of and allowing you to work more efficiently. Working as a team and being able to plan things out and set things up to be able to work better is also another important aspect.
